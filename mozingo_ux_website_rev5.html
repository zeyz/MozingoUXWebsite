New to Websites? Start Here

Getting your first website can feel overwhelming — but we make it easy.

Whether you're just starting a business or updating your online presence, Mozingo UX walks you through every step. You don’t need to know anything about hosting, domains, or tech stuff — that’s what we’re here for.

Here’s How It Works

  • 1. We Help You Buy a Domain: Your domain is your website’s address (like yourbusiness.com). We’ll help you choose and register it — and it’s yours, fully owned.
  • 2. We Set Up Hosting: Hosting stores your website online. We help you select a trusted provider and handle the setup for you.
  • 3. You Stay in Control: Everything is registered in your name and email. You keep all ownership and access.
  • 4. We Design & Build: We start the design process once setup is complete, with your input along the way.
  • 5. We Launch & Handoff: We launch the site and give you all login credentials — you’re in full control of your online presence.

Why This Matters

Too many businesses lose access to their sites because someone else set it up and kept control. At Mozingo UX, we make sure you always stay in control. We just help you get there.
